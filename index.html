PAST CONTINUOUS

We use the past continous to talk about something in progress at a particular time in the past

Positive: Subject + was/were + verb-ing

I was studying for my presentation yesterday.

Negative: Subject + was/were + not + verb-ing

I was not playing video games.

Interrogative: Was/Were + subject + verb-ing?

Were you playing Fortnite yesterday without me?
